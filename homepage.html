Meet Our Founder

Rehoboth Zawadi

Rehoboth Zawadi is a visionary leader in agricultural innovation with over 15 years of experience bridging the gap between traditional farming practices and modern technology. Her passion for sustainable agriculture and rural development led to the creation of Farmeconix.

With a background in both agricultural economics and information technology, Rehoboth recognized the need for a platform that connects farmers with the tools and knowledge they need to thrive in today's challenging agricultural landscape.

"Our mission is to empower every stakeholder in the agricultural value chain - from smallholder farmers to agricultural students and tech innovators - to work together towards a more sustainable and profitable future for Kenyan agriculture."
